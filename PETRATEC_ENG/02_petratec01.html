ABOUT US

Global Testing Professional nstitute that Creates the Future with Customers

PETRATEC, which was established in 2009 with higher reliability and enthusiasm for structure testing tailored to the needs of customers, has grown into a representative private test institute in Korea that has obtained KOLAS international accredited testing institute certification.

PETRATEC has been working with researchers in each field to solve problems and work with them, providing test and evaluation services for various products and structures developed and produced in all industries, from construction to machinery, automobiles, ships and railways to aviation. Based on constant change and innovation, creative and positive thinking, and tireless challenging spirit, we will continue to grow into a global testing professional institute that creates the future with our customers with the best technology.

PETRA _ Test + Evaluation + Consulting

It’s made from first letters of PETRA and Test, Evaluation and Consulting, which means large rock, rock in Latin.

[PETRATEC]
It means to become a foundation company that trusts and depend on customers and is the basis for their development.
It has the intention of becoming a solid test research institute with rock-like trust and strong technical skills.

  • CUSTOMER

  • SOCIETY

    contribute to social safety through fair and reliable testing and research.

  • NATION

    Contribute to national development through the provision of high-quality testing and research services for new unstandardized technologies and new product.

VISION Global No.1 testing research company that provides the best technology, high reliability, and batch testing services by skilled talented people

Fostering challenging and creative talents that develop together

Creation of broad customer value through the best technology and batch processing service

Highly reliable global testing institute with fairness and objectivity

Business Principle

PETRATEC Co., Ltd. stipulates the overall testing-related tasks in accordance with international standards and practices, such as KS Q ISO/IEC 17025, and applies them to employees, consigned/cooperative institutions, customers, related to testing work, as prescribed in Article 23 of the Framework Act on National Standards, Article 16 of the Enforcement Decree of the same Act, and the Guidelines for Operation of the Accreditation System for International Accredited Institutions.
